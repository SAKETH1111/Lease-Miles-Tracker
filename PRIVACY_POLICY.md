# Privacy Policy for Lease Miles Tracker

**Effective Date: December 2024**

## Overview

Lease Miles Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Lease Information**: Start date, end date, starting odometer reading, total allowed miles, and cost per mile
- **Mileage Entries**: Odometer readings, dates, and optional notes
- **App Preferences**: Notification settings, warning thresholds, and display preferences

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any data that could personally identify you

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:
- Calculating mileage usage and remaining lease miles
- Providing alerts and notifications about your lease status
- Generating reports and export functionality
- Improving app functionality

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
- No cloud synchronization occurs without your explicit action

### Security Measures
- Data is encrypted using iOS system-level encryption
- Access is restricted to the app itself
- No network connections are made by the app

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.** Your lease and mileage information remains private and is never:
- Sold to third parties
- Shared with advertisers
- Transmitted to external servers
- Accessed by us or any third party

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. All functionality is provided using Apple's built-in frameworks.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Export**: Export your data to CSV format at any time
- **Delete**: Remove individual entries or reset all data
- **Backup**: Your data is included in standard iOS backups

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any personal information and is suitable for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's description on the App Store.

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store review system or the app's feedback mechanism.

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- Apple's App Store Guidelines
- iOS Privacy Requirements
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
